about me

Chiropractic Therapy Tailored to Your Needs

I’m Steffan Harris, a qualified chiropractor and sports massage therapist dedicated to helping people move better, recover faster, and perform at their best. I hold a Master’s degree in Chiropractic from the University of South Wales (Welsh Institute of Chiropractic) and a Level 4 Diploma in Sports Massage.

With over eight years of experience in sports therapy, I’ve worked with athletes at all levels—from grassroots to elite—across rugby, football, and lacrosse. My experience includes working with the Newport Dragons, the South African Rugby squad in 2020, the U21 Welsh football team, and currently, Wales Lacrosse as we prepare for the upcoming World Championships.

Alongside my work in sports, I also practice at a clinic in Stroud, offering chiropractic care and soft tissue therapy for anyone looking to relieve pain, restore movement, and improve overall well-being. Whether you’re an athlete or simply want to move more comfortably, my goal is to provide expert, hands-on treatment tailored to your needs.
