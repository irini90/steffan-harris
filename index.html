services

Comprehensive Care Tailored to Your Needs

I treat pain and improve mobility through tailored chiropractic care and soft tissue therapy, helping you move better, feel stronger, and live pain-free. I start with a full consultation to assess your condition and outline a care plan. Treatments combine chiropractic adjustments and deep tissue massage to improve mobility, reduce tension, and enhance function. If needed, I provide a simple rehab plan to support your recovery, with regular check-ups helping to maintain long-term results.

Deep Tissue

Targets deeper muscle layers to relieve tension, improve circulation, and aid recovery.

Trigger Point Therapy

Focuses on releasing tight muscle knots to reduce pain and restore movement.

Chiropractic Adjustments

Uses precise spinal manipulations to improve alignment, mobility, and overall function.

Muscle Release Techniques

Combines pressure and movement to break down adhesions and enhance flexibility.

Muscle Stretching Techniques

Improves range of motion, reduces stiffness, and helps prevent injuries.

Instrument Assisted

Uses specialised tools to break down scar tissue, improve circulation, and promote healing.
